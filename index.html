Ad Blocker Blocker Blocker (ABBB)


br>

A simple system for getting rid of the dreaded "Ad blockers violate YouTube's Terms of Service" screen.




Why does this exist?

YouTube did a thing where they blocked Invidious from accessing the data of YouTube, because Invidious pretends to be a YouTube Android App, to access videos. YouTube finally realised what Invidious was doing, and all instances are blocked as of writing on the 31/03/2024 (03/31/2024 stupid format). This tool took almost 3 hours to make, and a Chrome extention is coming soon.

How to Install

As previously mentioned, a Chrome extention is coming soon, so for now, you have to create a bookmark and paste in the following text for the URL:

javascript:(window.location.href.includes("www.youtube"))?location.href=window.location.href.replace("www.youtube.com","abbblocker.github.io"):console.log("not a youtube page")and it should work. All you have to do is go on a youtube watch page and click the button. It also works on the homepage, but if you click it, I think it leads to this page idk.
